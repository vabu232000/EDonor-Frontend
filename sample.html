E-DONOR

Your kindness is someone's hope

E-donor is a platform that brings the phase 'Charity begins at home' to life. Using this website from the comfort of your home,you can selflessly render your respective services towards any noble cause for the welfare of people with disabilities, destitutes and orphans. We have several NGOs registered with us and its your choice to choose whom to donate and what to donate.The NGOs can create posters for their campaign using the templates offered by our website and post them on our social forum. Donors can like, comment, react and donate to the posts and also get their feedbacks from the respective NGOs. We hope to bridge the gap and connect the NGOs and the donors through our website.
